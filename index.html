My Process

Frames of Feeling

Connection First - Every great frame starts long before the shutter clicks. I spend time getting to know you: coffee chats, a call, even a quick walk so when the camera comes out, you're already at ease and the moments feel natural.

Guided but Natural

I'll give soft prompts, walk closer, whisper a secret, hold that laugh just enough to draw out genuine emotion. No stiff poses, no forced smiles, only movements that feel like you.

Light & Atmosphere

I chase the kind of light that tells a story on its own: warm sunset haze, moody twilight, city-glow at night. It's how I create frames that look like stills from a favorite film.

How I Hold a Story

Every couple moves to a rhythm that's completely their own. My job is to listen for it: the quiet laughs, the pauses, the way you hold each other and let it guide every frame. I'm there to observe, to anticipate, to catch the fleeting moments you don't even notice until you see them frozen in a photograph. Real will always matter more to me than perfect.
